What is Google Apps Script?

Apps Script is Google’s scripting platform to automate tasks across Workspace (Sheets, Gmail, Drive, Docs, etc.). It runs in the cloud, so there’s no server to maintain, and it’s perfect for lightweight EC automations.

  • Connect Shopify with Sheets/Drive/Gmail easily
  • Schedule jobs or react to webhooks
  • Keep running costs low
